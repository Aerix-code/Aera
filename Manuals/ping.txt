PING - Test network connectivity

SYNOPSIS:
    ping <host> [-c <count>] [-t] [-w <timeout_ms>]

DESCRIPTION:
    Sends ICMP echo requests to a host to test network connectivity.

OPTIONS:
    -c <count>      Number of ping packets to send (default: 4)
    -t              Continuous ping until stopped with Ctrl+C
    -w <timeout_ms> Timeout per ping in milliseconds (default: 1000)

EXAMPLES:
    ping google.com
        Sends 4 pings to google.com

    ping 8.8.8.8 -c 10
        Sends 10 pings to 8.8.8.8

    ping example.com -t
        Continuously pings example.com until Ctrl+C is pressed

    ping example.com -c 5 -w 2000
        Sends 5 pings with a timeout of 2000ms each
